Privacy Policy

for IELTS® ChatterCards

Effective Date: August 30, 2025
Last Updated: August 30, 2025

Introduction

IELTS ChatterCards ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our iOS mobile application IELTS ChatterCards (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Audio recordings or speech data
  • Any information transmitted to external servers

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

IELTS ChatterCards stores the following data locally on your device only:

Practice Data:

  • Speaking practice session history and duration
  • Cue card completion status and progress
  • Personal notes and preparation materials you create
  • App preferences and settings
  • Practice streaks and achievement status

Audio Recordings:

  • Voice recordings are stored locally on your device only
  • Recordings are never transmitted or uploaded anywhere
  • You have full control to delete recordings at any time
  • Audio data remains completely private to you

How We Use Information

The locally stored practice data is used solely to:

  • Track your speaking practice progress
  • Display your practice statistics and streaks
  • Show achievement progress and milestones
  • Provide personalized practice recommendations
  • Enable you to review your recorded practice sessions

Data Storage and Security

  • All practice data and recordings are stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage
  • No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties
  • Your recordings and practice data remain under your complete control
  • All data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app
  • You can manually clear all data through the app settings

Third-Party Services

IELTS ChatterCards does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app operates entirely offline after download.

Children's Privacy

IELTS ChatterCards is designed for language learners of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults. All practice data and recordings remain local to the device.

Your Privacy Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, you have complete control over your information:

  • All data stays on your device
  • You can delete practice data and recordings through the app
  • You can reset all app data by deleting and reinstalling
  • No account creation or data sharing required
